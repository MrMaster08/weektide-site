Get Tempo for Mac

Prefer not to wait for the App Store? Download directly — free.

Download Tempo.dmg
  1. Open the DMG and drag Tempo into Applications.
  2. First launch only: right-click Tempo → Open → Open. macOS shows a one-time notice because this direct build is signed with a developer certificate rather than distributed through the App Store. It opens normally ever after.
  3. That's it — create a sync account in Settings → Sync whenever you want your data on more than one device.
